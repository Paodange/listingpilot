Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 1, 2025  ·  Operated by Depeng Liu

This Privacy Policy explains how ListingPilot ("we", "us", or "our"), operated by Depeng Liu, collects, uses, and protects your information when you use https://cc.ntotech.top.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account data: your email address and a hashed (bcrypt) password when you register.
  • Usage data: a daily count of how many listings you have generated, stored against your user ID.
  • Payment data: subscription status and a subscription ID from DodoPayments. We never see or store your card details — payment is processed entirely by DodoPayments.
  • Product input data: the product name, features, and audience you submit when generating a listing. This data is sent to the DeepSeek API to generate your listing and is not stored by us after the response is returned.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and maintain your session.
  • To enforce daily usage limits based on your plan.
  • To process subscription upgrades and downgrades via DodoPayments webhooks.
  • To generate product listings via the DeepSeek AI API.

3. Third-Party Services

  • DeepSeek API — receives your product input to generate listings. Governed by DeepSeek's Privacy Policy.
  • DodoPayments — processes subscription payments. Governed by DodoPayments' Privacy Policy.
  • Google Sign-In (optional) — if you choose to log in with Google, we receive your email address from Google. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on a private server. Passwords are hashed with bcrypt and never stored in plain text. We use HTTPS for all data in transit. We do not sell your data to third parties.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, email us at support@ntotech.top and we will process the request within 30 days.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at support@ntotech.top. If you are located in the EU/EEA, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

7. Cookies

We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. Your authentication token is stored in your browser's localStorage solely to keep you logged in.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect when it was last revised. Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

For any privacy-related questions: support@ntotech.top
